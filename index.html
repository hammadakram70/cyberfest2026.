About Cyber Fest

Pakistan’s premier cybersecurity event — connecting students, professionals, and innovators to build a safer digital future.

🛡
About Cyber Fest

Cyber Fest is an annual cybersecurity event dedicated to empowering students and professionals with cutting-edge knowledge, hands-on experiences, and real-world insights. From capture-the-flag challenges to expert-led panels, Cyber Fest is where Pakistan’s digital defenders come together to learn, compete, and grow.

🚀
About Tech Hierarchy

Tech Hierarchy is a student-led technology community committed to advancing digital literacy, cybersecurity awareness, and innovation across Pakistan. With a mission to bridge the gap between academia and industry, Tech Hierarchy hosts world-class events, workshops, and networking opportunities that shape tomorrow’s tech leaders.

Our Journey

Event History

From a small gathering to Pakistan’s most anticipated cybersecurity event.

2024 — Edition 01
The Beginning

Tech Hierarchy launched the first Cyber Fest, bringing together 200+ students and professionals for a day of cybersecurity awareness, expert talks, and community building in Lahore.

2025 — Edition 02
Growing Stronger

The second edition expanded with 350+ attendees, industry-sponsored CTF challenges, and a formal speaker panel featuring top cybersecurity professionals from across Pakistan.

2026 — Edition 03
Bigger Than Ever

Cyber Fest 2026 is the largest edition yet — 500+ expected attendees, elite speakers, and a full day of high-value programming at Garrison University Lahore.
