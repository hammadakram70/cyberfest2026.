Who We Are

About Cyber Fest

Pakistan’s premier cybersecurity event — connecting students, professionals, and innovators to build a safer digital future.

About Cyber Fest

Cyber Fest is an annual cybersecurity event launched in 2025 by Tech Hierarchy. Now in its second edition in 2026, Cyber Fest continues to empower students and professionals with cutting-edge knowledge, hands-on challenges, and real-world industry insights. From Capture the Flag competitions to expert-led panels, this is where Pakistan’s digital defenders come together to learn, compete, and grow.

About Tech Hierarchy

Tech Hierarchy is a student-led technology community committed to advancing digital literacy, cybersecurity awareness, and innovation across Pakistan. With a mission to bridge the gap between academia and industry, Tech Hierarchy hosts world-class events, workshops, and networking opportunities that shape tomorrow’s tech leaders.

Our Journey

Event History

From a bold first step in 2025 to Pakistan’s most anticipated cybersecurity event in 2026.

2025 — Edition 01
The Beginning

Tech Hierarchy launched the inaugural Cyber Fest in 2025, bringing together students and professionals for a day of cybersecurity awareness, expert talks, and community building at Garrison University Lahore.

2026 — Edition 02
Bigger & Bolder

The second edition of Cyber Fest arrives in 2026 — spanning two full days and featuring 500+ expected attendees, elite speakers, industry-sponsored CTF challenges, workshops, and a newly expanded programming lineup.
